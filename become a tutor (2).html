To become a tutor on our website, follow these step-by-step instructions:

Create a Profile

Get started by filling out a form with your full name,contact details (email and phone number), and relevent qualifications or certifications to create your comprehensive profile

Choose your Subjects and Expertise

Choose subjects aligned with your expertise and teaching experience, including academic subjects, languages, test preparation, music, and more, to showcase your effective tutoring skills.

Describe Your Teaching Experience

Share a concise overview of your teaching experience, including years of tutoring/teaching, age groups taught, and notable achievements, enabling students to grasp your teaching background and expertise.

Set Your Availability

Specify your availability for tutoring sessions by indicating your preferred days and times to teach, enabling us to match you with students seeking lessons during your desired time slots.

Highlight Your Teaching Approach

Provide a glimpse into your teaching philosophy by describing your tutoring style and approach, highlighting unique methodologies/strategies that ensure effective learning outcomes for students.

Await Our Reply

After submitting your profile, sit back and relax. Our team will review your application and contact you soon. We may reach out via phone or email to further discuss

Complete Verification Process

For authenticity and quality assurance, we may conduct additional verification steps such as qualification checks, background checks, or references from previous students/employers—rest assured, we'll guide you through the process if necessary.

Start Tutoring

Start tutoring once your verified profile is complete; we'll match you with students based on their preferences, location, and your subject offerings. Be prepared to communicate with potential students, schedule lessons, and deliver high-quality instruction to help them achieve academic success.

Remember, creating a detailed and informative profile will significantly increase your chances of attracting students. Highlight your qualifications, experience, and teaching approach to stand out from other tutors. Good luck, and we look forward to welcoming you as a tutor on our website!
